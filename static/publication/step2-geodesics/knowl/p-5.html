Surjective isometric embeddings behave in the Carnot group case similarly as they do in the normed-space case. Namely, isometries between arbitrary (open subsets of) Carnot groups are affine [12], i.e., compositions of left translations and a group homomorphisms. For globally defined isometries, there is an even more general result that isometries between connected nilpotent metric Lie groups are affine [9].
