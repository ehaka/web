Let \((G,d)\) be a stratified group of step 2 equipped with a homogeneous distance \(d\) whose projection norm is strictly convex, and let \(\gamma\colon[0,\infty)\to (G,d)\) be an infinite geodesic.

Let \(d_\ell\) be the length-metric associated to \(d\text{.}\) By Lemma 6.1, the curve \(\gamma\) is also a geodesic of \((G,\norm{\cdot})\text{,}\) where \(\norm{\cdot}\colon V_1\to\RR\) is the sub-Finsler norm of the sub-Finsler metric \(d_\ell\text{.}\) Moreover by Lemma 6.1iii the norm \(\norm{\cdot}=\norm{\cdot}_d\) is by assumption strictly convex.

Consequently by Theorem 1.1, the geodesic \(\gamma\) is a line.
