Fix \(0\leq a\lt b\lt \infty\text{.}\) By assumption \(u_{\dilationfactor_k}\to \tilde{u}\) in \(\mathrm{L}^2([a,b];V_1)\text{,}\) so there exists some error term \(\epsilon\colon \NN\to V_1\) with \(\lim\limits_{k\to\infty}\epsilon(k)=0\) such that

\begin{equation} \intavg_{a}^b\tilde{u}(t)\,dt = \intavg_a^bu(\dilationfactor_kt)\,dt +\epsilon(k) = \intavg_{a\dilationfactor_k}^{b\dilationfactor_k}u(t)\,dt+\epsilon(k)\text{.}\tag{4.3} \end{equation}

The right-hand integral average can further be expressed as a difference of integral averages as

\begin{equation} \intavg_{a\dilationfactor_k}^{b\dilationfactor_k}u(t)\,dt = \frac{b}{b-a}\cdot\intavg_{0}^{b\dilationfactor_k}u(t)\,dt-\frac{a}{b-a}\cdot\intavg_{0}^{a\dilationfactor_k}u(t)\,dt\text{.}\tag{4.4} \end{equation}
