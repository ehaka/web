Let \(\pi_{V_1}\colon \mathfrak{g}=V_1\oplus\dots\oplus V_s\to V_1\) be the projection with respect to the direct sum decomposition. Then

\begin{equation*} \norm{X}_d\leq d(e,\exp(X+Y))\quad\forall X\in V_1,\,\forall Y\in [\mathfrak{g},\mathfrak{g}]\text{,} \end{equation*}

so the horizontal projection \(\pi = \pi_{V_1}\circ\log\colon (G,d)\to (V_1,\norm{\cdot}_d)\) is a submetry.
