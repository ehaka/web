By Lemma 6.1, the distance between the two lines in the group \(H\) grows sublinearly. Since the map \(\varphi\) is an isometric embedding, also the distance between the image lines in the group \(G\) grows sublinearly. Hence applying Lemma 6.1 in the converse direction implies that \(Y=Z\text{.}\) That is, the vector \(Y\in V_1^G\) does not depend on the point \(h\in H\text{,}\) only on the vector \(X\in V_1^H\text{.}\)
