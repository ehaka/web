Lemma 3.3.

For any vector \(v\in V_1\text{,}\) the Hamiltonian vector field of the normal left-invariant Hamiltonian \(h_{v,-1}\) has in left-trivialized coordinates the expression

\begin{equation*} \vec{h}_{v,-1}(\lambda) = \sum_{1\leq i\leq r}\lambda((L_{\pi(\lambda)})_*[v,X_i])\partial a_i + (L_{\pi(\lambda)})_*v\in T_\lambda(T^*G)\text{.} \end{equation*}
