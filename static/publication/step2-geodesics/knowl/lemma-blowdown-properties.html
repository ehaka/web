Lemma 2.14.

Let \(\gamma\colon [0,\infty)\to G\) be an infinite geodesic and let \(\tilde{\gamma} = \lim\limits_{k\to\infty}\gamma_{\dilationfactor_k}\) be any blowdown of the curve \(\gamma\text{.}\) Let \(u\) and \(\tilde{u}\) be the controls of the curves \(\gamma\) and \(\tilde{\gamma}\) respectively. Then

  1. The curve \(\tilde{\gamma}\) is an infinite geodesic.
  2. Up to taking a subsequence, the dilated controls \(u_{\dilationfactor_k}\) converge to the control \(\tilde{u}\) in \(\Lloc([0,\infty);V_1)\text{.}\)
