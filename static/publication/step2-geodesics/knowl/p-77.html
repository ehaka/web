The duality (3.8) will now be deduced by comparing the expressions (3.9) and (3.11). For an arbitrary vector \(w\in T_\lambda(T^*G)\text{,}\) let \(W\) be the left-invariant extension to a vector field on the cotangent \(T^*G\text{.}\) That is, the vector field \(W\) has a constant coefficient coordinate expression

\begin{equation} W=\sum_{i=1}^nw_i\partial a_i + \sum_{i=1}^nx_iX_i\text{.}\tag{3.12} \end{equation}
