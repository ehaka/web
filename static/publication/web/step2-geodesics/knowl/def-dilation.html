Definition 2.2.

A dilation by a factor \(\dilationfactor\in\RR\) on a stratified group \(G\)Â is the Lie group automorphism \(\delta_{\dilationfactor}\colon G\to G\) defined for any \(X=X_1+\dots+X_s\in V_1\oplus\dots\oplus V_s\) by

\begin{equation*} \delta_{\dilationfactor}\exp(X_1+X_2+\dots+X_s) = \exp(\dilationfactor X_1 + \dilationfactor^2X_2+\dots+\dilationfactor^sX_s)\text{.} \end{equation*}
