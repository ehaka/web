Let \(\mathfrak{g}\) be a Lie algebra. Let \(\delta\in\der(\mathfrak{g})\) be a diagonalizable derivation and let \(X\in\mathfrak{g}\) be an eigenvector of \(\delta\text{.}\) Then

\begin{equation*} \Ad_{\exp(X)} \circ \delta \circ \Ad_{\exp(-X)} = \delta - \ad_{\delta(X)}\text{.} \end{equation*}
