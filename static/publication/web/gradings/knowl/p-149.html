Following the strategy outlined in Section 3.7 of [22], we classify torsion-free gradings, i.e., gradings that admit a torsion-free realization, in nilpotent Lie algebras of dimension up to 6 over \(\mathbb{C}\text{.}\) The main part of the classification is the construction of a maximal grading (Algorithm 3.12) and the enumeration of torsion-free gradings (Proposition 2.25). Here we will give a brief overview of the gradings of each Lie algebra.
