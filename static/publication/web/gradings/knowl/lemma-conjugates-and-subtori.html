Lemma 2.15.

Let \(\mathfrak{t}_1\) and \(\mathfrak{t}_2\) be two split tori of \(\der(\mathfrak{g})\) with respective induced \(\mathfrak{t}_1^*\)-grading \(\mathcal V\) and \(\mathfrak{t}_2^*\)-grading \(\mathcal W\text{.}\)

  1. If there exists an automorphism \(\Phi \in \Aut(\mathfrak{g})\) such that \(\Phi \circ \mathfrak t_1 \circ \Phi^{-1} = \mathfrak t_2\text{,}\) then \(\mathcal V\) and \(\mathcal W\) are equivalent.
  2. If \(\mathfrak t_1 \subset \mathfrak t_2\text{,}\) then there exists a homomorphism \(f\) such that \(\mathcal V = f_* \mathcal W\).
