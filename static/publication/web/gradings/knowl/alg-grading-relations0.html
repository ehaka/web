Item 1.
Let \(\Omega = \{\alpha_1,\dots,\alpha_N \}\) be the set of weights of \(\mathcal V\) and let \(\{X^j_{m_{j-1}+1},\ldots,X^j_{m_j}\}\) be a basis of \(V_{\alpha_j}\) for each \(j=1,\ldots, N \text{.}\) Compute the structural coeffients, that is for every \(\alpha_i,\alpha_j\in\Omega\text{,}\) find the constants \(c_{l,k}^{p}\) given as follows
\begin{equation*} [X_l^i,X^j_k]=\sum_{p=m_{i+j-1}+1}^{m_{i+j}} c_{l,k}^{p}X_p^{i+j}\subset V_{\alpha_{i+j}} \end{equation*}
for any \(l=m_{i-1}+1,\ldots,m_k\) and \(k=m_{j-1}+1,\ldots,m_j\text{.}\)
