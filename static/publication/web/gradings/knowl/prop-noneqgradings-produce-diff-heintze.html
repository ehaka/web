Proposition 4.10.

Let \(\mathfrak{g}\) be a nilpotent Lie algebra and \(\alpha, \beta \in \der(\mathfrak{g})\) diagonalizable derivations with strictly positive eigenvalues. If \(\alpha\) and \(\beta\) define isomorphic Heintze groups, then they define equivalent \(\mathbb{R}\)-gradings.
