The quasi-isometric classification of Heintze groups reduces to the study of so called purely real Heintze groups, for which the associated derivation has real eigenvalues. Note that diagonal Heintze groups form a subset of purely real Heintze groups. By [9] the quasi-isometric classification problem of diagonal Heintze groups reduces to an algebraic problem of finding all the possible derivations defining non-isomorphic Heintze groups.
