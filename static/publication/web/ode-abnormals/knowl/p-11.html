Let \(G\) be a Carnot group of rank \(r\text{.}\) Then there exists a Carnot group \(F\) of rank \(r\) and a surjective Carnot group homomorphism \(F\to G\) such that for all abnormal curves \(\alpha\) and \(\beta\) in \(G\) the concatenation \(\alpha\star\beta\) lifts to an abnormal curve in \(F\text{.}\)
