Lemma 3.2.

Let \(G\) be a Carnot group of step \(s\) and let \(1\leq m\lt s\text{.}\) Let \(\gamma\colon (a,b)\to G\) be a horizontal curve whose closure \(\overline{\gamma((a,b))}\subset G\) contains the identity \(\identity{G}\) of the group \(G\text{.}\) If there exists a covector \(\covector\in\mathfrak{g}^*\) such that \(\abnormalpolynomial{X}{\covector}\circ\gamma\equiv 0\) for all \(X\in \lielayer{\mathfrak{g}}{m}\) and at least one of the abnormal polynomials \(\abnormalpolynomial{X}{\covector}\) is nonzero, then the curve \(\gamma\) is abnormal.
