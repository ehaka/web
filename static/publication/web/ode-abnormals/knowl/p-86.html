Let \(\hallset\) be the deg-left-right Hall set on the free Lie algebra of rank \(r\text{.}\) Let \(Q\in\polyring{\hallset}\) be a nonzero polynomial containing only variables of degrees at most \(m-1\text{.}\) Then there exists a step \(s\geq m\) and a covector \(\covector\in\freelie{r,s}^*\) such that \(Q\) is a common factor of all abnormal polynomials \(\abnormalpolynomial{X}{\covector}\) for \(X\in\freelielayer{r,s}{m}\text{,}\) and at least one of these abnormal polynomials is nonzero. Moreover, the step \(s\) only depends on the rank \(r\text{,}\) the integer \(m\text{,}\) and the degree of \(Q\text{,}\) but not on the polynomial \(Q\) itself.
