Let \(\freelie{r,s}\) be the free nilpotent Lie algebra of rank \(r\) and step \(s\text{.}\) Let \(m\in\NN\) and let \(\mathfrak{g}\) be the stratified Lie algebra defined by quotienting \(\freelie{r,s}\) by the ideal \([\lowercentralseriesterm{\freelie{r,s}}{m},\lowercentralseriesterm{\freelie{r,s}}{m}]\text{,}\) where \(\lowercentralseriesterm{\freelie{r,s}}{m} = \freelielayer{r,s}{m}\oplus \freelielayer{r,s}{m+1} \oplus \cdots \freelielayer{r,s}{s}\) is the \(m\text{:}\)th term of the lower central series of \(\freelie{r,s}\text{.}\) Then in the Carnot group \(G\) with Lie algebra \(\mathfrak{g}\text{,}\) every abnormal polynomial \(\abnormalpolynomial{X}{\covector}\) with \(X\in\lielayer{\mathfrak{g}}{m}\) depends only on the variables of degrees \(1,\ldots, m-1\text{.}\)
