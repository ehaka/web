Lemma 3.1.

Let \(\hallset\) be a Hall set for the free Lie algebra \(\freelie{r}\) of rank \(r\) and let \(X_1,\ldots,X_r\) be the generators of \(\freelie{r}\text{.}\) Then for every collection of polynomials \(Q_{1},\ldots,Q_{r}\in\polyring{\hallset}\) there exists a polynomial \(Q\in\polyring{\hallset}\) such that \(X_iQ = Q_{i}\) for all \(i=1,\ldots,r\text{.}\)
