Let \(P\colon G\to TG\) be a polynomial vector field in a Carnot group \(G\) of rank \(r\) and let \(P=\sum_{i=1}^nP_i\tilde{X}_i\) be its expansion in a left-invariant frame \(\tilde{X}_i\) of vector fields adapted to the stratification on \(G\text{.}\) Suppose that the polynomials \(P_i\colon G\to\RR\) for the horizontal components \(i=1,\ldots,r\) have bounded degree \(\deg(P_i)\leq d\text{.}\) Then all the horizontal trajectories of the ODE \(\dot{x} = P(x)\) lift to abnormal curves in the free Carnot group \(\freecarnot{r,s}\) of rank \(r\) and step \(s\text{.}\)
