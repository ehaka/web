Let \(P\colon G\to TG\) be a polynomial vector field in a Carnot group \(G\) of rank \(r\text{.}\) Then there exists a Carnot group \(F\) of rank \(r\) and a surjective Carnot group homomorphism \(F\to G\) such that horizontal trajectories of the ODE \(\dot{x} = P(x)\) lift to abnormal curves in \(F\text{.}\)
