We present our method of classifying gradings explicitly in the simple case of the Lie algebra \(L_{4,2}=L_{3,2}\times\mathbb{R}\) given in the basis \(Y_1,\ldots,Y_4\) with the only bracket \([Y_1,Y_2]=Y_3\text{.}\) The maximal grading is over \(\mathbb{Z}^3\) with the layers

\begin{align*} V_{(1,0,0)}\amp=\langle Y_1\rangle,\amp V_{(0,1,0)}\amp=\langle Y_2\rangle,\amp V_{(1,1,0)}\amp=\langle Y_3\rangle,\amp V_{(0,0,1)}\amp=\langle Y_4\rangle\text{.} \end{align*}
